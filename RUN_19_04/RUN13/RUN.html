COVID19 NATIONAL DATA, NATIONAL/REGIONAL TRENDS AND ESTIMATIONS.

FITTING DATA = TOTAL CASES = POSITIVES + RECOVERED + DEATHS

FITTING LOCATIONS: REGIONS AND PROVINCES

This report is related to data processing on the spread of COVID-19 in Italy and single regions/provinces. Data are provided by the Italian Civil Protection:

"https://github.com/pcm-dpc/COVID-19"

This report is not intended to be official or definitive in terms of the included estimation/forecasts. The models are not predictive-type, but rather equations describing the data available until this moment. Their purpose is to assess the current trend, so that any acceleration or deceleration of the infection can be deduced. In general, for each data sample a fixed time window is selected and used to estimate the model (fitting). After which results are compared with the previous and/or following days.

-The fit is exponential/linear (Curve 1 ) and logistic/sigmoidal (Curve 2). Estimations are performed with the aim of verifying near which curve the future points are placed in order to evaluate at a glance the possible variation of the spread speed.

Equations: Curve1 EXP(x) = a*exp(b*x) + c;

Curve2 SIG(x) = a/((exp(b*(c+x))) + a1*exp(b1*(c1 + x)) - d)

Algorithms: Non Linear Least Square or Least Absolute Residuals

Models and reports are under continuous review.
