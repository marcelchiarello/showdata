COVID19 GROWTH FACTORS AND LETHALITY REPORT.

This report is related to data processing on the spread of COVID-19 in Italy and single regions/provinces. Data are provided by the Italian Civil Protection:

"https://github.com/pcm-dpc/COVID-19"

GROWTH FACTOR [%] = (TOTAL CASES (k) - TOTAL CASES (k-1)) / TOTAL CASES (k - 1) | k = actual day; k - 1 = previuous day | for each day(k)

LETHALITY [%] = TOTAL DEATHS (k) / TOTAL CASES (k) | for each day(k)
